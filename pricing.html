Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, absolutely. There are no contracts or commitments. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time, and you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
What happens to my words if I downgrade?
You keep all your words and their processed data. You simply won't be able to add more than the free limit (5 words/month) or access Pro-only learning modes until you upgrade again.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your data is stored securely on AWS infrastructure with encryption. See our Privacy Policy for full details on how we handle your information.
What languages are supported?
Vocab Master is designed for English vocabulary learning. All content (definitions, examples, nuances) can be translated to 50+ native languages so you can learn in the way that's most effective for you.
Can I use it on mobile?
Yes! Vocab Master is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means it works great on any device with a modern browser. You can add it to your home screen for an app-like experience.
